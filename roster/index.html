Frequently Asked Questions

Is this a Heroplan.io Alternative?

Yes! With the permanent shutdown of Heroplan, Empuzzled's Roster Manager serves as a comprehensive alternative to Heroplan.io. You can continue tracking your Empires & Puzzles heroes, limit breaks, emblems, and costumes safely and directly within your browser.

Can I import my data from Heroplan.io?

Unfortunately, because the Heroplan site was shut down permanently and its AWS drives were deleted, there is no way to export data from the old site anymore. You can quickly recreate your roster manually here and keep your data safely managed moving forward.

What happened to Heroplan.io?

The owner of Heroplan.io had to safely close the site down entirely as they were personally losing money every month maintaining the servers and could not pay for another month. Empuzzled’s hero tracker aims to be the best available alternative for everyone in the community.
