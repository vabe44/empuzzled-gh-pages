Released in 2024, he heals your team for a massive 1400 boosted HP while throwing sand, water, and fire at your enemies. Is he a confusing mess or a tactical genius? Mana Optimization Strategy

How it works

By default, Craum has Average mana speed, requiring 10 tiles to charge. Equipping troops with a Mana Bonus can reach specific Breakpoints — the exact percentages where your tile count drops — allowing your hero to fire their special skill faster, with less tiles. Additionally, as a Druid, this hero can unlock a +4% Mana Bonus through Emblems (Talent Tree). Also, with the Costume Bonus active, they gain an extra +5% Mana Bonus. These bonuses can significantly lower the troop level required to reach these breakpoints.
