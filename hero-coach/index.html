Frequently Asked Questions

What is the Hero Coach event in Empires & Puzzles? ▾
Hero Coach is a recurring event that lets you instantly max a Legendary hero to 4/80 (or 4/90 with limit break) using Gems. The hero must be at least 2 years old and not already maxed. No ascension materials or leveling time required. Browse eligible heroes in the Hero Database.
How much does Hero Coach cost in gems? ▾
Hero Coach costs vary based on the hero. A base Legendary hero typically costs around 300–500 gems. Costumes add additional gem costs. The exact pricing depends on the specific hero and any active promotions.
Which heroes are eligible for Hero Coach? ▾
Only Legendary (5★) heroes that are at least 2 years old and not already at max ascension are eligible. This includes heroes from older portals, Season 1–6 heroes, and event heroes that meet the age requirement.
Is Hero Coach worth the gems in Empires & Puzzles? ▾
Hero Coach is most worth it for players who lack ascension materials or want to quickly power up a strong older hero. It saves months of leveling time and rare ascension mats. Compare the gem cost against summoning odds and material farming time. Also consider alternatives like Soul Exchange and Fated Summon for acquiring heroes.
