Frequently Asked Questions

How do I get an Empires & Puzzles 9th Anniversary creator code?
Content creators will be releasing giveaway videos between March 18 and March 24, 2026. Watch their videos (embedded above) and follow their individual community instructions to enter and win one of their 50 gift codes!
What are the rewards for the 9th Anniversary?
Creator codes give 999 Gems, 3x Rare Master Trainers and 1x Tome of Golden Emblems. Alternatively, winning the Official SG Raffle yields massive rewards, such as 9,999 Gems and Legendary Tomes of XP!
Can I win more than once?
Usually, similar events cap rewards ensuring multiple identical gift codes cannot be redeemed on the same account. However, you can enter multiple creator giveaways or possibly pass duplicated codes along to your alliance friends!
Are there any other redeem codes?
Absolutely! If you want more rewards, make sure to check out our Empires & Puzzles Redeem Codes page. We update it frequently with all the newest promo codes that gives extra avatar pins, free energies, and gems!
When does the Empires and Puzzles 9th Anniversary event happen?
The official 9th Anniversary week celebration and content creator giveaways typically run from March 18 to March 24, 2026. Make sure to claim your free redeem codes before they expire during this celebration window!
Are there free gems given during the Empires and Puzzles birthday event?
Yes! Players can win up to 9,999 free gems from the official Small Giant (SG) Raffle. On top of that, every creator gift code offers an additional 999 free gems. It is arguably the best time of the year to stock up on premium currency.
